    The best 10 seconds I spent on my Kanban board setup

    3 minute read

    Flipping your Kanban board columns is a cheap way to reduce multitasking and stress. Eventually, it contributes to a more sustainable pace.

    2 weeks ago, I blogged about how seemingly small changes can have big consequences. Here is another experiment that could have great consequences.

    The problem with current Kanban boards
